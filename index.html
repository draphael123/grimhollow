GRIMHOLLOW

A sorceress descends into the sunless crypt. Wade into the dead, delete the pack, take what drops — and dive deeper.

Left-click — move, or hold on a foe to cast Firebolt
Right-click — Frost Nova (freezes & shatters the crowd)
Spacebar — dodge roll toward cursor (brief invulnerability)
I — inventory & gear · click a bag item to equip
